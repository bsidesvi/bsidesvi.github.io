Custom Sponsorship

Security B-Sides encourages participation from a wide range of organizations. If you have an idea for custom sponsorship, please inform us so that we can evaluate and determine if it is a viable option. These ideas may include, but are not limited to, the contribution of:

- After Party Sponsor
- Speakers Party Sponsor

Depending on the level of contribution, custom sponsorship could include any of the benefits from the sponsorship packages listed above but would include, at a minimum, the following recognition:

- Logo on the event website
