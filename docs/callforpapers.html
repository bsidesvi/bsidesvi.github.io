Call for Papers




Hey, what's up? Do you love hacking stuff and teaching others how to do it? Do you have a wicked idea that you want to share with a bunch of smart people? If yes, then you should totally apply to speak at BSides Vancouver Island 2023, a one-day, one-track event that connects the local security community on October 13th, 2023 in Victoria, BC.

BSides Vancouver Island is a rad and safe place where you can share your thoughts, teach unusual topics, or just tell fascinating security stories. Submit your paper if you think you have a killer topic that you want to share with the Vancouver Island Security Community. We're looking for speakers who can rock the stage for 45 minutes in person, showing off some cool technical tricks of cybersecurity that will blow the minds of our audience of seasoned pros. No boring sales pitches, please. Just pure, awesome content.  Unfortunately, we can't pay for your travel or accommodation costs, but we can help you with visa letters if needed.  We appreciate every submission and we're stoked that you're interested in BSides Vancouver Island 2023.

We want to let you know that topics of interest don't have to be limited to a specific security domain. They can also help the community to get a better picture of what's happening around us and the challenges faced by government, industry and academia at home and abroad in the security industry.

Here are some examples of topics that might interest us:
•    Internet of Things (IoT)
•    Cryptocurrency / Blockchain
•    Dark Web / OSINT
•    Generative AI / LMM
•    5G / IoT / Edge / Cloud Security
•    Zero Trust Security Model
•    Digital Privacy and Cyber Resilience
•    Red Team and Blue Team
•    Digital Forensics / Incident Response
•    Cyber Threat Intelligence / Malware Analysis
•    AppSec / DevSecOps
•    Fun War Stories / Panel Discussions
