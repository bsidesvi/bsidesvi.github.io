Call for Volunteers




Hey, are you looking for a fun and rewarding way to get involved in the cybersecurity community? Do you want to meet new people, learn new skills, and contribute to a great event? If so, you should consider volunteering for BSides Vancouver Island!

BSides Vancouver Island is a one-day conference that brings together security enthusiasts, professionals, researchers, and hackers from all over the island and beyond. It's a place where you can share your ideas, network with peers, and discover new opportunities. And it's happening on October 13th, 2023 at the Victoria Conference Centre.

But we can't do it without your help. We need volunteers to assist with various tasks before, during, and after the conference. Whether you want to help with registration, speaker coordination, logistics, social media, or anything else, we have a role for you. No experience is necessary, just a willingness to learn and have fun.

As a volunteer, you'll get free admission to the conference, a cool t-shirt, lunch and snacks, and a chance to win some awesome prizes. You'll also get to meet some of the speakers and organizers, and make new friends in the community. Plus, you'll gain valuable experience that you can put on your resume or portfolio.

Just fill out this form below and we'll get back to you soon with more details. Thank you for your interest and support. We hope to see you at BSides Vancouver Island!
