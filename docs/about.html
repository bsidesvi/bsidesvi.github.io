About BSide Vancouver Island


Yo, listen up! BSides Vancouver Island (BSidesVI) is the ultimate annual shindig for all you security buffs and anyone curious about diving into the security scene.

Picture this: a massive hub of learning, mingling, and teaming up. The talks and presentations at BSidesVI aren't your grandma's boring lectures – we're all about sharing ideas, giving high-fives for feedback, and dishing out knowledge to everyone, whether you're a total newbie or a seasoned pro.

And here's the scoop:

  • The backstory? The very first Security BSides Vancouver Island went down at the University of Victoria, and it pulled in speakers from every nook and cranny of the world. Yep, we're talking global superstars on our stage!

But wait, there's more:

  • The conference cranks up the energy with an atmosphere that's more electric than a lightning storm. You'll walk away with a brain stuffed full of cybersecurity wisdom and a bunch of new pals.

  • BSidesVI is your chance to rub elbows with fellow cool cats, swap stories, and build a network that's tighter than a bungee cord.

  • We're all about creating a vibe where newbies and pros can shoot the breeze, grow together, and have a blast while doing it. It's like one big, awesome fam coming together. So, what are you waiting for? Join the party!
