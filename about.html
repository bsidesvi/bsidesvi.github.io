BSides Vancouver Island (BSidesVI) is an annual conference for security practitioners and those interested in (or looking to enter) the field.

Our event is a source of education, communication, and collaboration. The technical and academic presentations at BSidesVI are given in the spirit of peer review and for the dissemination of knowledge among all specialties.
